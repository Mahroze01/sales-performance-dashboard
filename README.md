## Project Overview

This project analyses retail sales data using Microsoft Excel and Power BI to identify trends in sales performance, profitability, customer behaviour, and product performance. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and data-driven recommendations.

This project was created as part of my Data Analytics portfolio to demonstrate practical skills in business intelligence, data visualisation, and analytical reporting.

## Business Problem

A retail company wants to better understand:

- Which regions generate the highest sales and profit.
- Which products contribute the most to revenue and profitability.
- Which customer segments are the most valuable.
- Whether discount strategies negatively impact profitability.
- Which product categories should receive greater business focus.

The goal is to support better business decisions using data.

## Project Objectives

- Clean and validate retail sales data.
- Build interactive dashboards in Excel and Power BI.
- Analyse regional sales performance.
- Identify the most profitable products.
- Compare customer segments.
- Evaluate product category profitability.
- Investigate the relationship between discounts and profit.
- Present clear business recommendations.

## Dataset

The project uses the **Sample Superstore** retail dataset containing approximately **10,000 transactions**.

Key fields include:

- Region
- Customer Segment
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Sales
- Quantity
- Discount
- Profit

## Business Questions Answered

- Which region generates the highest sales?
- Which region generates the highest profit?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which customer segment contributes the most profit?
- Which product category is the most profitable?
- Do larger discounts reduce profitability?

## Key Findings

- The West region generated the highest sales and overall profit.
- Technology was the most profitable product category.
- Furniture generated high sales but significantly lower profit margins.
- Phones generated the highest sales revenue.
- Copiers generated the highest overall profit.
- Discounts above approximately 30% were associated with negative average profit.


## Business Recommendations

- Continue investing in high-performing Technology products.
- Review pricing and discount strategies for Furniture products.
- Investigate the profitability of Tables and Bookcases.
- Focus future marketing campaigns on profitable customer segments.
- Carefully monitor high-discount sales to protect profit margins.
